Our Story

JonseyFBS Shop was created for fingerboarders who love the hobby and want quality parts to build and customize their setups. What started as a personal passion for fingerboarding grew into a small shop focused on providing decks, parts, and accessories for the community.

We believe fingerboarding is more than just a hobby — it’s creativity, progression, and fun. Our goal is to make it easier for riders to get great gear and keep pushing their skills.
